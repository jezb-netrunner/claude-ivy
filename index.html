Our Story

Claude & Ivy brings the charm of the City of Pines to the modern Filipino. We are passionate about curating bags that fit your life, whether you are conquering the daily grind or enjoying a laid-back afternoon.

To us, a bag isn't just something you carry; it’s a companion. It holds your essentials and tells your story. As we grow our roots in Baguio, we promise to keep bringing you styles that inspire confidence and celebrate your individuality.
