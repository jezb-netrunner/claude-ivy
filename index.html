Our Story

Rooted in
the City of Pines

Claude & Ivy brings the charm of Baguio to the modern Filipino. We curate bags that fit your life — whether you are conquering the daily grind or enjoying a laid-back afternoon.

To us, a bag is more than something you carry. It is a companion. It holds your essentials and tells your story. As we grow our roots here, we promise to keep bringing you styles that inspire confidence and celebrate your individuality.
